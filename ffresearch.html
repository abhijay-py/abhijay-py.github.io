    Fall Force Research

    Research into Predicting Falls

    This project is a research project on predicting falls using neural networks.

    This research was conducted as a part of the Biomedical AI Lab at the University of North Texas alongside Nora Xiao and Aditya Pujari.

    Falls are a leading cause of health problems among the elderly. This research study aimed to predict such falls to better assess fall risks to help the elderly and those with Parkinson's Disease.

    54 input variables were used to predict the x, y, and z components of the ground reaction forces. These were all put together in a three layer neural network. Subject-wise validation was used to better train the model.
